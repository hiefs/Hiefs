It's very lightweight, easy to understand and I am knowledgable of HTML, CSS, Markdown, and Typescript.

So far I am enjoying it. I need to figure out how I would like the content to be presented on the first page, and what other tabs I would like to have (game and book reviews) and present those that way. I like the way the blog posts are organized and presented so I am likely to leave it this way.

I don't like to think of them as reviews because I don't really aim to become a _game reviewer_ I just want to record my thoughts on the games that I ahve played/finished. I'll likely have scattered posts for the on-going titles like Soulframe or Final Fantasy XIV.
